The Artist

Ozzy Ninte
aka XTC Canten

From Berea's streets to Braamfontein's vibrant sound, XTC CANTEN (born Ozzy Ninte) is a South African trap artist who crafts sonic experiences that hit different. His music is a wild ride through the highs and lows of life — equal parts vamp, glamour, and street truth.

A founding member of the Valhala Cult collective, he's part of a tight-knit crew pushing boundaries and building their own lane. His debut album "Ghost of the Year" (2026) cements his position as one to watch.
